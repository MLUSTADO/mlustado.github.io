About

The little cafe, Java Been, has grown a reputation within the community. They serve people only the highest quality service and products every visit. Experience the difference.


The Owner - Miss Lucy Lemon


Meet Lucy Lemon, the passionate owner of a coffee shop that goes far beyond serving a simple cup of joe. Lucy's love for coffee is deeply rooted in her belief in the power of community and the importance of quality. With each sip, she strives to deliver an exquisite experience that will ignite your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Lucy understands that behind every coffee bean lies a story of hard work, dedication, and craftsmanship. From the farmers who meticulously nurture the plants to the roasters who artfully bring out the flavors, every step is crucial in creating a cup of perfection. That's why Lucy personally selects only the finest beans from sustainable and ethical sources, ensuring that each batch is a testament to the dedication of everyone involved in the process.

But it's not just about the coffee itself for Lucy; it's about the people. Her coffee shop is a haven that embraces all walks of life, welcoming everyone who enters with open arms. It's a place where strangers become friends and where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee creates an ambiance that feels like home.

Lucy's deep-rooted connection to her community drives her to make a difference. She supports local artists, musicians, and organizations, hosting events that celebrate their talent and contributions. Her coffee shop becomes a vibrant hub, buzzing with creativity, lively conversations, and laughter.
